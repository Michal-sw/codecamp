Nathan Drake

Sic parvis magna

Drake chasing his fortune

Born in 1976 as the second son of American archeologist, brother of Samuel Drake. In 1981, 5 years after Drakes birth his mother had been suffering from an unknown terminal illness, which led to her suicide. After that, he along with his brother found home in Saint Francis' Boy's Home, a Catholic orphanage. All of their mother's possesions were later sold by their father (who gave them up to the orphanage). The two spent the next few years in the orphanage, during which Nathan took interest in books and was taught Latin by the orphanage's nuns, all while learning free-running and climbing from Sam. Eventually, Sam was kicked out of the orphanage due to criminal activities, before taking a job to support both himself and Nathan.

Beggining his journey, Drake, an aspiring treasure hunter found himself stumbling on tips of the lost El Dorado treasure. With help from his lifelong friend Victor, and his love Elena, they began the search. However there was one man eager to outsmart them, Eddy Raja... An adventure full of epic moments, gunfights and cinematic moments.

Drake new journey began when he and his colleague Harry Flynn found a trace of the long forgotten Shambala. Epic cinematic moments, love struggles topped with witty humour. In the journey full of love, betrayal and friendship we follow Drakes footsteps uncovering one of the worlds scariest secrets.

Drake's third story finds its place in the hot african deserts, a town long buried in the sand, exotic culture and a promise of a rich treasure. Will Nathan be able to outsmart rich & powerful on the race to uncover Arabias' biggest treasure?

In the final chapter of Drake's storys, Nathan is long after his retirement, having established his marriage with Elena. Conflicted still thrill-searching Drake is fighting an internal struggle when a long lost friend enters his life... Will he resist the urge to leave the cozy and stable lifestyle?
